Welcome to MAZE SHOOTER a 2D multi-player overhead shooter.


In this world you will encounter the following elements.


Blocks: A breakable world element. Break these using the “shoot” or “punch” commands. Breaking blocks will earn you more bullets. You can purchase more bullets here get bullets.


Walls: Represents the edge of the playable map. You cannot destroy a wall


Rival Player: Represents another player sharing the map. You may attack rival players. Get them before they get you!


Monsters: Represents an enemy that may attack you.



Here is how to play:


Movement - use the arrow keys up, down, right, left


Shoot - use the “d” key or “space”


Punch - use the “f” key
